## Introduction ðŸ“

In **Bangladesh**, rural farmers often face **electricity shortages** that affect agricultural productivity. At the same time, reliance on **fossil-fuel-powered irrigation** creates environmental challenges and puts pressure on the **national electricity grid**.  

**Farmonergy** is designed as a **hardware-software integrated solution** that:

- Enables **farmers** to generate and monitor solar energy  
- Uses **IoT sensors, batteries, and smart devices** for real-time monitoring  
- Allows **automatic surplus electricity transfer** to the national grid  
- Supports **financial sustainability** through loans and government incentives  
- Provides **predictive maintenance** and operational transparency  

> With Farmonergy, rural agriculture can become **self-sufficient, sustainable, and profitable**, while contributing to **clean energy adoption** in Bangladesh.
